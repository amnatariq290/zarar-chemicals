Health, Safety & Environment Policy

Proactive Risk Management, Not Reactive Compliance

We are committed to conducting all operations in a way that protects our employees, contractors, and visitors, while minimizing environmental impact.

Safety Infrastructure On-Site

  • Active first-aid stations and mandatory PPE across all operations
  • On-site emergency response plans and fire safety drills
  • Spill kits positioned at all chemical handling points
  • Permit-to-work and lockout-tagout (LOTO) procedures
  • Incident and near-miss reporting culture

Environmental Controls

  • Wastewater from production/lab operations treated through licensed facilities
  • Hazardous vs. non-hazardous waste segregation with licensed disposal contractors
  • Energy and water consumption monitored as a baseline for reduction targets
  • Full GHS-compliant SDS with environmental hazard classification for every product
  • Compliance register maintained for all applicable environmental regulations

The Bigger Picture

Locally Owned. Internationally Aligned.

As one of Pakistan's first locally owned, ISO-aligned oilfield chemical manufacturers, Zarar Chemicals pays taxes locally, employs Pakistani chemists and engineers, and reduces national dependency on imported oilfield chemistry — without compromising on the international standards operators expect.
