Technology Highlights

Featured Chemistry

A closer look at three flagship formulations developed entirely in-house.

ZM600

ShaleGUARD-AM

Polyamine shale inhibitor. Cation-exchange technology displaces Na⁺/K⁺ on smectite clay surfaces with high-affinity polyamine groups, combining wellbore film formation with inhibited osmotic water uptake.

Stable to 150°C BHST
ZS102

STEELSAFE HT

Quaternary amine corrosion inhibitor for HTHP acid stimulation, formulated for high-temperature, high-pressure well environments.

NACE TM0172 >90% Inhibition @ 150°C in 15% HCl
ZS289

VexisFLOW

Zwitterionic viscoelastic surfactant (VES) that self-assembles into wormlike micelles for proppant transport — a polymer-free alternative that eliminates residual formation damage.

Polymer-Free · Zero Residue
